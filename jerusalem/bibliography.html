Bibliography
for the Kingdom of Jerusalem.

The Crusades scholarship in English has been substantially revised since the 1990s. Jonathan Riley-Smith was the dominant late-twentieth-century authority; Christopher Tyerman and Thomas Asbridge are the current generation's standard one-volume narratives. The Arab sources (Ibn al-Athir, Usama ibn Munqidh, Ibn Jubayr) are now extensively available in English translation.

A reader's reference.
